Larceny in Durham

Within this graph, we analyze the cases of reported larceny within Durham county from 2011 to 2016 shown by the heat map and each year from 2012 to 2016 shown by the red data points. Beyond looking at areas of high larceny activity (the most notable of which was the Southpoint Mall with 147 cases over the time period), we analyzed the two most efficient police station locations based on the coverage of the county (note that one is central and one is in far northern Durham, suggesting that there might be a more efficient means of police station placement), and the relative density of crime for each of the public and charter high schools in Durham county.
