Domestic Violence in Durham

Within this graph, we look at the cases of domestic violence over the period of 2011 to 2016 over the county to determine the areas of high density that could potentially be targeted for the development of domestic violence crisis shelters. We based the potential locations on the GPS coordinates of 40 local churches and focused attention on the availability of services as Durham county demonstrates a severe lack of resources for victims of domestic violence.
